About

The story.

What it is
Aquipax is the UK's first app to combine financial health, physical health, and mental health in a single platform. It gives users their safe-to-spend number — the money left after every bill, subscription, and commitment — alongside a personalised meal planner, activity tracker, sleep tracker, and mood journal.
Why it exists
Built for people who earn a decent wage but are always skint. Always waiting for payday. Sleeping badly, eating whatever's cheap, losing hope. Financial stress is a health crisis — and no app was treating the two as connected. Aquipax does.
Who it's for
UK households who want clarity, not complexity. No bank logins. No transaction categorisation. No Open Banking. Just your numbers — income, bills, goals — and one honest answer: what you can safely spend this month.
Company
JeanieIQ Ltd — UK-based technology company. Aquipax is available as a Progressive Web App at aquipax.com and as a native Android app. Built with Netlify, Supabase, Stripe, and Edamam. Launched 2026.
