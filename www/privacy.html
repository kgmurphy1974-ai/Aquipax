Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Aquipax by JeanieIQ

Aquipax is built by JeanieIQ, a technology company based in Rotherham, South Yorkshire. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

Who we are

Data controller: JeanieIQ, Rotherham, South Yorkshire, England.
Contact: hello@jeanieiq.com

What data we collect

Account data — your email address and encrypted password, used to identify your account and keep you signed in.

Financial planning data — income, bills, renewals, savings goals and holiday budgets that you enter into the app. This data is yours and is used only to calculate your safe-to-spend number and provide personalised guidance.

Receipt data — when you scan a receipt, the image is sent to our AI service (Anthropic) for processing. We store only the extracted text data (items, prices, store name) — never the original image.

Ask Jeanie conversations — questions you ask Jeanie are sent to OpenAI for processing along with your financial context. Conversations are not permanently stored on our servers beyond your current session.

Usage data — basic anonymised analytics such as which features are used. No personal identifiers are attached to this data.

What we do NOT collect

  • Bank account details, sort codes, or card numbers
  • Government identifiers (National Insurance number, passport etc.)
  • Precise location data beyond what you voluntarily enter
  • Contacts, photos or files beyond receipts you choose to scan
  • Data from children — Aquipax is not designed for users under 18
  • Advertising or behavioural tracking data

How we use your data

  • To provide the Aquipax service — calculating your safe-to-spend number and storing your financial plan
  • To sync your data across your devices when you are signed in
  • To process receipts using AI (Anthropic API) — receipt images are processed and immediately discarded
  • To power the Ask Jeanie assistant (OpenAI API) — your question and financial context are sent to OpenAI and not retained after processing
  • To send you account-related emails (signup confirmation, password reset)
  • To improve the app through anonymised usage analytics

Legal basis for processing

We process your data under contract (to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interests (to improve the product and provide personalised guidance). Where we ask for consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

Who we share data with

Supabase — our database and authentication provider (hosted in EU/Ireland). Stores your account and financial data securely with row-level security.

Anthropic — processes receipt images via AI. Images are not retained after processing. Anthropic privacy policy →

OpenAI — processes Ask Jeanie questions along with your financial context. Data is not used to train OpenAI models under our API agreement. OpenAI privacy policy →

Netlify — hosts the Aquipax web application and serverless functions.

Stripe — processes subscription payments if you upgrade to Plus or Family. We never see your full card details. Stripe privacy policy →

Apify — used to fetch live supermarket prices when you scan a receipt. Only product names are sent — no personal data. Apify privacy policy →

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Anonymised analytics data may be retained longer for product improvement purposes.

Your rights under UK GDPR

Access
Request a copy of all data we hold about you
Rectification
Correct inaccurate data we hold
Erasure
Delete your account and all data (in Settings)
Portability
Export your data as JSON (in Settings)
Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests
Restrict
Limit how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@jeanieiq.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Aquipax uses only essential cookies required for authentication and app function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Financial data is encrypted at rest in Supabase. Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data.

ICO registration

JeanieIQ is registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) as required under UK data protection law. If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact & complaints

For any privacy concerns, contact us at hello@jeanieiq.com.
