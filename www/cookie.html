Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Aquipax by JeanieIQ

This policy explains how Aquipax uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our app and website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use a web app. They help the app remember information about your visit — such as whether you're signed in.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies only. Aquipax uses only the cookies that are strictly necessary to make the app work. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or analytics cookies that identify you personally.

Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the app to function. You cannot opt out of these without the app breaking.

  • Authentication session — set by Supabase to keep you signed in across page loads. Expires when you sign out or after 7 days of inactivity.
  • App preferences — stored in localStorage (not a cookie, but similar) to remember your settings and financial data locally on your device.
  • Cookie consent — a small flag in localStorage that records whether you've acknowledged this policy.

What we do NOT use

  • Advertising or targeting cookies
  • Third-party analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking of any kind

Third-party services

Some third-party services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Stripe — if you visit the Stripe payment page to subscribe, Stripe may set cookies on their domain. These are governed by Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Supabase — our authentication provider may set session cookies. These are essential for keeping you signed in.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from staying signed in to Aquipax. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies stored on your device
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when cookies are being set

Changes to this policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at hello@jeanieiq.com.
