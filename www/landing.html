Questions

The honest FAQ.

Does it connect to my bank?
No — and on purpose. Aquipax doesn't read your transactions. You tell it your bills and income once; it does the maths from there. No Open Banking, no third-party data sharing, no "we couldn't reconnect your account" emails.
How is this different from YNAB, Emma, or Snoop?
Those tools are powerful but they assume you want to categorise every transaction. Aquipax assumes you don't. It gives you a single forward-looking number — what you can safely spend this month — and gets out of the way.
Is it really UK-specific?
Built for it. UK fuel prices, UK supermarket comparisons (Tesco, Sainsbury's, ASDA, Morrisons, Waitrose, Aldi, Co-op, Iceland and more), council tax in the calendar, MOT and renewal tracking, sterling everywhere. Not a US app with a £ sign painted on.
What does the 7-day free trial include?
Full access to everything in your chosen plan — Plus or Family — for 7 days. No credit card required. After 7 days, you'll be asked to subscribe or you'll drop back to the free tier. Your data is always yours.
Do I need to download anything?
No. Aquipax is a Progressive Web App. Open it in your browser, tap "Add to Home Screen" on iPhone or Android, and it behaves exactly like a native app — with your icon, fullscreen, offline support, and push notifications.
Is my financial data safe?
Yes. Your data is stored securely in your personal account with row-level security — no one else can access it. Receipt images are processed by AI and never stored. We don't sell data, we don't run ads. The product is the product.
