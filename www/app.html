Aquipax
User Guide
1
🏠 Set up your home screen
When you first open Aquipax, you’ll see your safe to spend number at the top. This is the money left after every bill, subscription and commitment this month.

Tap Financial Health to enter your income and bills. The more you add, the more accurate your number becomes.
2
💷 Enter your income
Go to Financial Health → Bills tab → Income.

Enter your monthly take-home pay (after tax). If you have a partner, add their income too. Use your actual bank deposit amount, not your salary.
3
🔄 Add your regular costs
Regular Monthly Costs — everything that goes out every month: rent/mortgage, council tax, energy, broadband, phone, subscriptions, insurance, gym, Netflix, etc.

Tap the quick-add buttons to add common bills in seconds. You can also import from a bank statement to auto-detect your bills.
4
🛒 Add variable costs
Variable costs are things that change month to month: food shopping, petrol, eating out, clothes, kids’ activities. Add your typical monthly budget for each.

These are estimates — they don’t need to be exact. They help Aquipax give you a realistic safe-to-spend number.
5
📅 Annual costs
Bills that hit once a year — car insurance, MOT, Christmas, holidays, home insurance. Enter the annual amount and Aquipax smooths them monthly so you’re never caught out.
6
⛽ Fuel prices
Tap Directory in the sidebar, then Fuel. Allow location access and Aquipax shows you the cheapest fuel stations near you, updated live from the UK Government API.
7
🔄 Switch & Save directory
Tap Directory in the sidebar. Browse by tab: Finance (insurance, credit cards), Utilities (energy, broadband), Fitness (supplements, gyms), Health (therapy).

All links are tracked via Awin — if you switch and save, you’re helping keep Aquipax free.
8
😊 Meal planner
Go to Physical Health → Nutrition tab → Meal planner.

Set your dietary preferences first (vegetarian, gluten-free etc.), then tap Generate plan. Aquipax builds a personalised 7-day plan using your calorie target from your health profile.

Tap Swap on any meal to change it. Tap ☆ Favourite to save meals you love.
9
🛒 Shopping list
The shared shopping list lets every family member add items throughout the week. Go to Physical Health → Nutrition → Shopping list.

Tick items off as you shop. Mark items as 🏠 Have it if you already have them at home — they’ll be excluded from the basket price comparison.
10
❤️ Physical health tracking
Steps — tap Start counting in the Activity tab. Keep the screen on for best accuracy.

Weight & BMI — log your weight in the Body tab. Aquipax tracks your trend over time.

Water — tap the glasses in the Nutrition tab to log daily water intake.
11
🧠 Mental health features
Mood tracker — log how you feel each day (1–5). Your mood history is shown as a 7-day chart.

Sleep tracker — tap Wake up when you get up and log your hours. Quality is scored based on duration.

Gratitude journal — log 3 things you’re grateful for each day.

Breathing exercise — box breathing: 4 seconds in, 4 hold, 4 out, 4 hold. Reduces stress in minutes.

Financial stress score — automatically calculated from your real financial data. No questionnaire needed.
12
👥 Family plan & referrals
Tap Refer in the sidebar to get your unique referral link. Every 3 friends who sign up earns you 1 month free. No cap — keep referring, keep earning.

The Family plan lets up to 4 household members share one subscription. The shopping list, calendar and financial notes are all shared in real time.
13
🤖 Ask Jeanie
Tap the blue ? button (bottom right) to open Ask Jeanie — your AI financial assistant. Jeanie knows your actual numbers and can answer questions like:

“Can I afford a holiday?”
“Why is my number so low this month?”
“How can I save more?”

Jeanie gives personalised answers based on your real data, not generic advice.
14
🏆 Achievements
Earn badges as you use Aquipax — for setting up your bills, logging your first mood, reaching step goals, building your emergency fund and more. Tap the 🏆 Achievements strip on the home screen to see all 12 badges.
